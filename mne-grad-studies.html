Graduate Studies

M.S. & Ph.D. Degrees in Mechanical and Nuclear Engineering

The Mechanical and Nuclear Engineering Department offers an outstanding graduate education experience, and all of our programs are of critical importance to the future health and safety of our world. Our students work directly with renowned experts who are currently pursuing successful careers in the fields of Mechanics, Materials, Thermal Fluid Systems, and Nuclear Engineering around the nation and world.

Not only do we offer a unique, first of its kind, hybrid Ph.D. in Mechanical and Nuclear Engineering, we also offer a traditional Master of Science with either a thesis or non-thesis option. Additionally, we offer an online M.S. in Mechanical and Nuclear Engineering.
