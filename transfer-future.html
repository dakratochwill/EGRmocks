Scholarships and Financial Aid

We’re here to help you finance your education at VCU Engineering. Our flagship program is the Wright Engineering Access Scholarship, which offers undergraduates a low- or no-debt path to a high-value degree. We also have an array of merit-based and need-based funding opportunities.

Visit VCU

Don’t take our word for it. Come and see what VCU Engineering is all about. We have information sessions Monday through Friday at 9:15 a.m. and 1:30 p.m. These sessions include a tour of our facilities. Check out the university’s open houses to learn more about VCU’s multifaceted campus. Prefer a customized individual or group visit? Contact us and let’s set it up.
